PRIVACY POLICY DOCUMENT
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Last Updated: May 30, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws.

2. DATA COLLECTION
We collect the following types of personal data:
- Contact information (name, email, phone)
- Financial information for processing payments
- Usage data for improving our services

3. DATA RETENTION
Personal data is retained for the following periods:
- Customer data: 7 years after account closure
- Financial records: 10 years as required by law
- Marketing data: Until consent is withdrawn

4. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER GDPR
Under GDPR Article 15, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Erase data (right to be forgotten)
- Data portability
- Object to processing

5. DATA PROTECTION OFFICER
Contact our DPO at: dpo@company.com

6. COMPLIANCE
This policy complies with:
- GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act)

For questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@company.comPRIVACY POLICY DOCUMENT
